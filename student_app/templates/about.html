About Us

Welcome to Study Planner — a simple, student-friendly platform built to help you organize your academic life and mental well-being. Our goal is to empower students to manage assignments, schedules, and wellness in one space, reducing stress and improving productivity.

Whether you’re tracking deadlines, setting up a study plan, or writing your thoughts in a diary, we’re here to support your journey. Let’s study smarter, not harder.
